rtc-asr benchmark deck

Low-latency ASR, built for turn-taking.

This GitHub Pages front end turns checked-in benchmark artifacts into a benchmark product page: who answers fastest, who closes fastest, who keeps tails tight, and which lane gives the cleanest accuracy without pretending blocked runs are shippable results.

The comparison view stays focused on measured ASR lanes with committed JSON artifacts. That means operators can compare low-latency behavior directly across REST, websocket partials, websocket finals, runtime profile, and error rates without mixing in unpublished blockers.
